## Overview

Developed a reliability testing platform at Lumiphase AG for characterizing photonic components under accelerated stress conditions. The project encompassed instrument selection, integration, coding measurement routines, and designing custom PCBs for sample fixation.

## Architecture

Python scripts orchestrate SCPI-controlled instruments (source meters, temperature chambers) via GPIB and USB. Custom KiCad PCBs provide mechanical fixation and electrical routing for the photonic samples under test.

## Challenges

- Minimizing contact resistance variation between test runs
- Ensuring thermal uniformity across the sample area
- Calibrating measurement routines against known reference standards

## Lessons Learned

Instrument automation with SCPI over GPIB is powerful but brittle - building retry logic and state validation into measurement scripts prevents silent data corruption.
